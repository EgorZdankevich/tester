1.Summary - A short description of the problem that clearly indicates the cause and type of error.
2.Project - Name of the tested project
3.Application Component - Part or function name of the product under test
4.Version number - The version on which the bug was found
5.Severity - The most common is a five-level defect severity grading system:

S1 Blocker
S2 Critical
S3 Major
S4 Minor
S5 Trivial
6.Priority - Defect priority:

P1 High
P2 Medium
P3 Low
7.Status - Bug status. Depends on the procedure used and the bug life cycle
8.Author - Bug report creator
9.Assigned To - Name of the person assigned to resolve the issue
